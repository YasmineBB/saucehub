Ancho


This is a lovely mild, earthy sauce that can also be used as a flavourful marinade.


You will need:


  • 4 Dried Ancho Chillis
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 50ml Vinegar
  • 175ml of Water
  • Â½ tsp Oregano

Instructions:

  1. Toast the chillis in a pan on medium heat for 1 minute
  2. Soak the chillis in hot water for 30 minutes, making sure they are covered
  3. Chop up the onion and garlic and blend alongside the soaked chilled and wet ingredients until a smooth consistency is formed
